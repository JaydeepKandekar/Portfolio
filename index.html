ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Jaydeep Kandekar, hailing from a small town in Maharashtra, India. I hold a bachelor's degree in Science with a major in Physics and a specialization in Astrophysics from Savitribai Phule Pune University. Currently, I am pursuing a Master's in Astrophysics at Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich, Germany.

My journey in the world of astrophysics has been both exciting and fulfilling. From studying the cosmic mysteries at the university to exploring the vastness of the universe, my passion for the stars continues to grow.
